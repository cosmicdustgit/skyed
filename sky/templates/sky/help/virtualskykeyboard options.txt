
Keyboard shortcuts:

a ? toggle atmosphere
g ? toggle ground
h ? change map projection
i ? invert colours 
,? toggle Ecliptic line 
;? toggle Meridian line 
e? toggle Ra/Dec gridlines 
z? toggle Az/El gridlines
m ? toggle Galactic gridlines
M ? toggle Galactic plane
q ? toggle cardinal points
s ? toggle stars
S ? toggle star labels
u ? toggle planet/Sun/Moon labels
p ? toggle planets/Sun/Moon 
o ? toggle planet orbits
c ? toggle constellation lines
v ? toggle constellation names
b ? toggle constellation boundaries
R ? toggle meteor shower radiants
8 ? set time to now
j ? decrease time speed
k ? set time rate to zero
l ? increase time speed
- ? subtract 1 day
= ? add 1 day
[ ? subtract 1 week
] ? add 1 week
% ? rotate left
' ? rotate right
& ? increase magnitude limit
( ? decrease magnitude limit
